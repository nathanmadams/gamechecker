Surrender and kickout

Surrender

You can concede games and leave them :

  • During the first restaurant setup : this will cancel the whole game (no need to start a broken game), or
  • Starting from the 5th turn if you are not the leading player

To leave a game, just click the 'resign' button during your Working day phase :

This feature is not allowed by default and needs to be turned on at game setup.

Kickout

You can now define a "kickout" time at game setup. Be careful, you won't be able to change it afterwards. Each player (including you) exceding this time will be kicked out of the game.

Both of these features can lead to unexpected results, so please tell me if you have issues.

Kicked out players are replaced by a 'FcmBot', which does nothing (it just passes). This behavior could be improved. Things could be handled differently, and I might update this "bot" later (it has nothing to do with a bot yet, but let's call it a bot)
